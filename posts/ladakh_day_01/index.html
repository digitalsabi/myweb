Trip to Ladakh

Published on October 01, 2020

Hello, I am Sabiha, a Mumbaiker Travel Blogger. This is my first post and what better way to kick start than by posting about the most beautiful place I have seen till date.

Day 1

I have just landed at my dream place, Leh, the capital of Ladakh and my abode for the next few days. I am so excited to share all of this with you folks!

Oh my my.. This place is like no other. The beauty of it can’t be expressed in words but I’ll still try. The mountains, rivers, valleys and lakes are so mesmerising and are no less than heaven. This amazing place is making me hum these lines..

Yeh Haseen Vadiyaan, Yeh Khula Aasmaa, Aa gaye hum kahaan, ooo mere saajna

This is just the beginning. So stay tuned for some some amazing posts about my journey in this heaven on earth!
